**AI Remote Sensing Tasks:**

- **Classifying:** Land use, vegetation types, soil conditions
- **Detecting:** Objects (ships, aircraft, buildings), anomalies, changes
- **Segmenting:** Pixel-level mapping of features
- **Predicting:** Crop yields, weather impacts, urban growth
- **Monitoring:** Environmental changes, disaster progression
- **Estimating:** Biomass, population density, pollution levels
